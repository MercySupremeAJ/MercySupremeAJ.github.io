Welcome to my digital space! I am a **Data Scientist & Computer Vision Researcher** obsessed with representation learning theory, 3D volumetric segmentation, and pushing the boundaries of what neural networks can see. 

This repository contains the source code for my interactive digital portfolio: **`mercy_portfolio.html`**.

## About My Portfolio
I wanted a portfolio that reflects my love for beautiful, complex architecture—both in deep learning and in design!

When you open `mercy_portfolio.html`, you'll see what I call my "Luminous Glassmorphism" interface. Instead of a flat, boring background, I built a fully 3D animated "Holo-Lotus" entirely out of CSS. The geometric petals slowly rotate in 3D space, glowing and pulsing with vibrant Pink, Violet, and Cyan gradients behind frosted glass panels. 

Inside the panels, you'll find everything you need to know about my journey: from my Distinction MSc in Data Science and my time as a Completions Specialist at SLB, to my hardcore research on Hybrid 3D U-Nets (AFW-NET) for medical imaging!
